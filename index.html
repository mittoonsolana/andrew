About This Coin

$ANDREW is a community-driven tribute token created to honor Staff Sgt. Andrew Wolfe, a National Guard member who was critically wounded in the recent D.C. attack. This project was built with a simple mission: turn collective support into something meaningful.

Our model is transparent — 80% of all creator-fee revenue will be used to buy back $ANDREW and send those tokens to the wallet dedicated to supporting Andrew Wolfe, while the remaining 20% is allocated to responsible project growth and promotion.

$ANDREW isn’t just another meme token; it’s a symbolic gesture from the crypto community to show solidarity, respect, and appreciation for someone who served. The community decides how far this goes — our role is to keep everything clear, fair, and mission-focused.
